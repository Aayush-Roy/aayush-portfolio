## Overview

**Sohwais** is a traditional clothing e-commerce platform built on the MERN stack, with a royal heritage look and feel brought to life with Framer Motion animations. It's designed to give the admin full control over the store — products, orders, and customer enquiries — all from a dedicated dashboard.

## Key Features

### **Storefront**

- **Men's & Women's Collections**: Browse traditional clothing organized by category and collection.
- **Smooth, Eye-Catching UI**: A royal heritage aesthetic brought to life with Framer Motion animations throughout the browsing experience.
- **Enquiry Collection**: Visitors can raise product enquiries directly from the storefront.

### **Admin Dashboard**

- **Product Listing**: Create and manage clothing products and collections for men and women.
- **Order Management**: Track and manage customer orders and view full order details.
- **Coupon Codes**: Create discount coupons with limited-time validity.
- **Enquiry Management**: View and respond to customer enquiries from one place.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB
- **Caching**: Redis, for fast data access across the storefront and dashboard
- **Media Storage**: Cloudinary
- **Payments**: Razorpay
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
